# Effects of 5GHz Cell Tower on Human Body at 30ft

# Introduction

Exposing a human subject to an RF field of strength 3,000-15,000 uW/m2 should trigger rouleaux formations.

Grounding should resolve them.

# Methods

Expose the human to the high-strength RF field, take a blood sample, ground, sample, re-expose,
take a sample.

## Protocol

### First Exposure

Expose the subject to the RF field for ten minutes.

Take a blood sample.

### Ground

Subject sits on grounding mat with substantial (~1 sq ft) skin contact for ten
minutes. (This is happening within the RF field.)

Take a blood sample.

### Second Exposure

Subject gets off mat, waits another ten minutes.

Take a blood sample.
