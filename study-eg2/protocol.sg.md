# Protocol Summary

Expose the human to the high-strength RF field, take a blood sample, ground, sample, re-expose,
take a sample.


# Steps

## First Exposure

Expose the subject to the RF field for ten minutes.

Take a blood sample.

## Ground

Subject sits on grounding mat with substantial (~1 sq ft) skin contact for ten
minutes. (This is happening within the RF field.)

Take a blood sample.

## Second Exposure

Subject gets off mat, waits another ten minutes.

Take a blood sample.


# Actions

## Take Blood Sample

- use lancet to prick finger
- allow a small droplet to form
- place on glass slide
- cover with glass cover
- find several central regions away from distorted edges to analyze and capture
  - photograph each
